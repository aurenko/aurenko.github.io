Interests

Sports

I like watching sports, particularly ice hockey and baseball. My favorite teams are Philadelphia Flyers for ice hockey and the Philadelphia Phillies for baseball. I also like the Philadelphia Eagles and the Philadelphia 76ers, who are the city's American football and basketball teams, respectfully. Philadelphia also has a Major League Soccer (MLS) team called the Philadelphia Union, but I do not watch them. In addition to Philadelphia sports, I also follow the Washington Capitals hockey team and the Washington Nationals baseball team, since they are the major league affiliates to minor league teams I like, the Hershey Bears of the American Hockey League and the Harrisburg Senators of the AA Eastern League of Minor League Baseball.

Video Games

My favorite videogame is Forza Horizon 4, which an open-world racing game. It would make sense if you look at my other categories.

Cars

I really like cars. My obession with them came from my younger brothe who is also into cars. I primarily like cars from since the 1980s onward because I think anything before that would be too old for my taste. My dream car would be a Ford Mustang because my Grandpa on my Mom's side had one from the 1960s/early 1970s in a color called "Aspen Gold". He died in 1992, 13 years before I was even born, in a work accident. It was such a shame because I would probably enjoy it.
